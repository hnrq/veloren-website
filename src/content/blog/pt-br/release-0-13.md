0.13 Release
------------

Veloren is releasing 0.13! This version is 5 months in the making, and we have a lot to show off!

If you're reading this before [18:00 GMT on July 23rd](https://everytimezone.com/s/020e183f), make sure to join the release party. There will be a custom pirate-themed map with plenty of secrets to find ( thanks to @Hannibal ), minigames, and other events so don't miss it and make sure to bring your friends along! üè¥‚Äç‚ò†Ô∏è Explore the handcrafted jungle isles to seek the treasures of the Sunken Duchess!
